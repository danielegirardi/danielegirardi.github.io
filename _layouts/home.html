My research focuses mainly on macroeconomics and political economy. Most of my work can be traced back to two main research projects. The first is concerned with the interaction between politics and markets. The second explores the macroeconomic effects of aggregate demand dynamics, with special emphasis on medium- and long-run effects.

I currently work as an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Massachusetts Amherst.
